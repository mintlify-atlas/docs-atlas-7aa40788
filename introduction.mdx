</Note>

## Why 'e' exists

In a world of complex software, bloated frameworks, and over-engineered solutions, 'e' stands as a monument to simplicity. Each implementation is a meditation on the fundamental question: **what is the essence of computation?**

The answer, as it turns out, is 'e'.
